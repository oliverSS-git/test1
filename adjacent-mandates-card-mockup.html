Deployment Familiarization — Perishable Skills at Home Station

AFSFC leadership has identified the core gap: Airmen don't touch deployment-relevant equipment until they arrive downrange. Radios, tactical comms, LOGDET equipment, land nav, buddy care. The platform provides home-station reps on low-density, perishable skills.
