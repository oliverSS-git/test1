Eligible Contracting Vehicle — Pilot

OTA — 10 U.S.C. § 4022
Other Transaction Authority enables rapid prototype execution without traditional FAR requirements. No large budget authority required for pilot phase. Appropriate for technology demonstration.

Eligible Contracting Vehicle — Scale

SBIR Phase II
If SSVR qualifies as a small business, SBIR Phase II provides a streamlined path from prototype to program of record. Pilot performance data serves as the technical baseline for Phase II application.

Budget Authority — Pilot Phase

Pilot phase structured to operate within existing O&M or RDT&E authority. No programmatic budget line required to begin. Assessment period determines follow-on resource requirements before any POM input is needed.

Acquisition Risk Profile

Minimal. Pilot is bounded in scope, duration, and cost. Documented off-ramp at Day 60. No vendor lock-in during pilot period. All data generated remains property of AFSFC regardless of outcome.
